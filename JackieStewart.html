SIR JACKIE STEWART - HELMET - 1969 - POSTER

Story Behind The Print

TRVL Art, Sir Jackie Stewart and Race Against Dementia presents The Flying Scot Collection TRVL Art has partnered with 3 times World Drivers' Champion Sir Jackie Stewart to celebrate his 80th Birthday and to commemorate his achievements as one of the most successful drivers in Formula 1 racing history. The Flying Scot Collection includes four Design Posters and one Fine Art Print. An expansion of Automobilist's signature Helmet Collection, this Design Poster features Sir Jackie's open face Buco helmet, haloed with the iconic Royal Stewart Tartan, on top of a Scottish Flag background.

  • Uniquely designed and created by Automobilist's team of artists
  • Uses the latest 3D modelling technology to rebuild the helmet from scratch
  • Comes as a standard sized poster, 50 x 70 cm (19¾ x 27½ inch), framing not included
  • Reproduced as a high quality offset print on 175g/m2 paper
  • Ships worldwide from South Africa, rolled and delivered in a protective tube
  • Official licensed product | Sir Jackie Stewart
  • By buying this product, you donate to Race Against Dementia, Sir Jackie's global charity on a mission to fund pioneering research into the prevention and cure of dementia
