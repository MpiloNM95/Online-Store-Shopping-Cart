WILLIAMS RACING - 750 GRANDS PRIX | COLLECTOR’S EDITION

Story Behind The Print

The Williams name is inextricably linked with Formula 1 history and even though family involvement has now been relinquished, the squad continues to fight with the ambition of rekindling its glory days. Fresh investment and a revitalized atmosphere are the perfect tools Williams Racing has in its armoury to re-emerge as a Formula 1 contender once more. This Collector’s Edition captures 3 iconic cars from the history of the team - the first, March-Ford 761, one of the most successful with Nigel Mansell at the wheel of an FW14B and the car representing the current season.

  • Specially designed and created on the occasion of Williams’ 750th race, Monaco Grand Prix 2021
  • Exclusive edition of 750 pieces only, uniquely numbered
  • Hot-foil white and silver embossing including the unique 750 Grands Prix logo
  • Uses the latest 3D modelling technology to design the poster
  • Comes as a standard sized poster, 50 x 70 cm (19¾ x 27½ inch), framing not included
  • Reproduced as a high quality offset print on 175g/m2 paper
  • Global Shipping from South Africa, rolled and delivered in a protective tube
  • Officially Licensed Product | Williams Racing
